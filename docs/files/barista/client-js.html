bbopx.barista.client

Let’s try and communicate with the socket.io server (Barista) for messages and the like--client-to-client communication.

There are two major categories: “relay” and “query”.  Relays are for passing information on to other clients (e.g.  “where I am”); queries are for asking barista information about what it might know (e.g.  “where is X”).
